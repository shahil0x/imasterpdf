Can I delete multiple pages at once?
Yes, you can delete any number of pages by specifying page numbers or ranges.

You must keep at least one page in the PDF. The tool will prevent you from deleting all pages.
Are my files secure?
Yes, all files are processed automatically and deleted after 30 minutes. We don't store or share your documents.
Can I delete pages from password-protected PDFs?
Yes, but you'll need to unlock them first using our unlock tool.
Is there a limit to how many pages I can delete?
No, you can delete as many pages as you want, as long as at least one page remains.
