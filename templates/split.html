Split Options

Enter page numbers or ranges separated by commas. Example: "1-3, 5, 7-9" will create three files: pages 1-3, page 5, and pages 7-9.
Examples:
• 1-5 (pages 1 through 5)
• 1,3,5 (pages 1, 3, and 5)
• 1-3, 7-9 (pages 1-3 and 7-9)
• 10- (from page 10 to end)

Each page will be saved as a separate PDF file. The files will be combined into a ZIP archive for easy download.
