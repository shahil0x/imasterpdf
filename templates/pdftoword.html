Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the conversion?
Our converter achieves 95%+ accuracy for digitally created PDFs. Scanned PDFs may require OCR processing.
What Word format is generated?
Files are converted to DOCX format, compatible with Microsoft Word 2007 and newer, and Google Docs.
Are my files secure?
Yes, all files are processed automatically and deleted after 30 minutes. We don't store or share your documents.
Can I convert scanned PDFs?
Yes, but for best results with scanned documents, ensure they have clear text and good resolution.
Is there a limit to file size?
Maximum file size is 50MB. For larger files, consider splitting first or using our premium options.
