What This Tool Does

Our PDF merger allows you to combine multiple PDF files into one cohesive document. This tool is perfect for:

  • Combining multiple chapters into a single book
  • Merging reports from different departments
  • Creating portfolios from individual pieces
  • Assembling legal documents and contracts
  • Organizing scanned documents into proper sequence

The merger preserves all original formatting, including:

  • Page orientation (portrait/landscape)
  • Image quality and resolution
  • Text formatting and fonts
  • Hyperlinks and bookmarks
  • Security settings and permissions

How to Merge PDF Files

Merging PDFs is simple with our intuitive interface:

  1. Upload Files: Drag and drop or select multiple PDF files (up to 20 files)
  2. Arrange Order: Use drag-and-drop to arrange files in your preferred sequence
  3. Preview: Check the page order and make adjustments if needed
  4. Merge: Click the merge button to combine all files
  5. Download: Get your single, merged PDF document

Additional features include:

  • Page-by-page rearrangement within files
  • Remove specific pages before merging
  • Add page numbers to merged document
  • Customize margins and spacing
  • Add table of contents automatically

FAQs

Q: How many PDFs can I merge at once?

A: You can merge up to 20 PDF files in a single operation.

Q: Will the merged file be larger than the sum of individual files?

A: Usually slightly smaller due to optimization, but never significantly larger.

Q: Can I merge password-protected PDFs?

A: Yes, but you'll need to unlock them first using our unlock tool.

Q: What if my PDFs have different page sizes?

A: Our tool automatically adjusts to maintain each page's original dimensions.

Q: Is there a page limit?

A: No hard page limit, but extremely large merges may take longer to process.
